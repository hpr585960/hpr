Haryana





speciality of Haryana



Haryana is well known for its cattle wealth and is the home of the famous Murrah buffalo and the Haryana cow. No wonder there is an abundance of milk and milk products in Haryanvi cuisine. People make butter and ghee at home and use these liberally in their daily diet.

Famous temple

Braha Sarovar in Kurukshetra and Sarveshwar Mahadev Temple. Pathar Masjid in Kurukshetra. Bhadrakali Temple. Gurudwara Nada Sahib in Panchkula. Mansa Devi Temple in village Bilaspur. Sheetla Devi Temple in Gurugram. Dargah of Bu Ali Shah Qalandar in Panipat. Baba Rameshwar Dass Temple in Karnal.

Hill Stations

Kasauli. The ideal choice for travellers staying in or close to Haryana is Kasauli. Morni Hills. View this post on Instagram. Parwanoo. Parwanoo is a scenic hill station which has perfect weather and will surely give you a wonderful experience. Chail. Barog.

Famous food

Kachri Ki Sabzi. Singri Ki Sabzi. Hara Dhania Cholia. Methi Gajjar. Kadhi Pakora. Mixed Dal. Khichri. Bathua Raita.
