The Lotus Temple

The magnificent Bahá'í House of Worship, or Lotus Temple, is another popular tourist destination in Delhi. Tourists consider it as an architectural masterpiece with nine sides and a stunning central dome. Further, you will be attracted to its architectural beauty even if you are not a religious follower. Apart from that, the white concrete and marble exteriors offer a resemblance to a white lotus. Moreover, nine pools of water surround this temple, enhancing its beauty.

Indian Spices

Delhi is the house for some of the biggest markets selling all kinds of Indian spices, known for having the most extensive taste palette. As Delhi is famous for its food, it knows all the spices. You will find all kinds of spices and mixes in Khari Baoli, a popular street in Delhi. It offers different kinds of spices and groceries.

Madhubani Paintings

Delhi is also popular for its elaborate art in the form of Madhubani paintings. These can be ideal when decorating houses and walls with Indian patterns, artwork and colours. Experts prepare these with stunning fabric colours before printing them onto silk, cotton, floral and other types of various fabrics. These mostly include beautiful Hindu themes, painting pictures of Krishna, Ram, Shiva, Durga and others.
