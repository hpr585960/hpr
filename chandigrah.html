Attraction for touristers

Sukhna Lake, Rock Garden, Government Museum and Art Gallery are major tourist attractions of Chandigarh. One unique feature in the layout of Chandigarh is its roads, classified in accordance with their functions. An integrated system of seven roads was designed to ensure efficient traffic circulation.
