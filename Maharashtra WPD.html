Maharashtra

Maharashtra is one of India's largest commercial and industrial centres, which has led to its being called the gateway of India. Maharashtra is also famous for its culture and beauty.
Maharashtra is the 3rd largest and one of the most populated states in India. Bordered by the Arabian Sea and the Western Ghats, it is a favoured destination for travel enthusiasts. Besides its world-famous entertainment industry, the state is also known for its delectable taste palate, rich culture, festivities, etc.

Mumbai

The capital city of Maharashtra has a nickname, “Manhattan of India”. Mumbai is a collection of seven islands. A few important places that must be on your "Mumbai Darshan" list are the Gateway of India, Elephanta Caves, Marine Drive, Markets of Mumbai, Siddhivinayak Temple, beaches of Mumbai, Basilica of Our Lady of the Mount and Fort Bassein.
