Goa





speciality of Goa



Goa is India's tropical paradise. Around 20% of the land in Goa falls into the beautiful Western Ghats of India, a vast mountain range and a treasure house of biodiversity. The forests here are teeming with exotic wildlife, including Indian giant squirrels, mongoose, Slender Loris, Indian macaques and sloth bears.
