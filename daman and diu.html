Electronics

The bazaar in Daman brim with shops that sell disallowed (by law) foreign goods at reasonable prices. Be it kitchen appliances like toasters and ovens, blenders, refrigerators, grillers, or home appliances such as televisions, stereo sets, mobile phones, and cameras. You name it; they have it.
