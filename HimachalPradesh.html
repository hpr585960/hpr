Himachal pradesh





speciality of Himachal pradesh



Himachal is well known for its handicrafts. The carpets, leather works, shawls, paintings, metalware and woodwork are worth appreciating. Pashmina shawl is one of the products which is highly in demand not only in Himachal but all over the country. Himachali caps are also famous artwork of its people.

Famous temple

BAJRESHWARI TEMPLE: Just outside the town of Kangra is the temple dedicated to Bajreshwari Devi. BAIJNATH: The ancient temple at Baijnath is particularly beautiful. JWALAMUKHI TEMPLE: Not too far from Kangra is this popular place of pilgrimage.
