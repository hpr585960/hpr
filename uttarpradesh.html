UTTAR PRADESH





speciality of uttar pradesh



Monument

Monuments of Uttar Pradesh includes beautiful cenotaphs, forts, walled cities, stupas and ancient ruins of Buddhism. The city homes several famous sites like
1. Taj Mahal.
2. Fatehpur Sikri.
3. Agra Fort.
4. Dhamek Stupa.
These monuments have also been declared as the World Heritage Sites.

Handicrafts

Uttar Pradesh is famous for handicrafts such as carpet weaving, hand printing, chikan (a type of embroidery), metal enameling, brocade and brass, and ebony work. UP also has the biggest brass and copperware manufacture area in India. The history of the State of Uttar Pradesh is very ancient and interesting.
